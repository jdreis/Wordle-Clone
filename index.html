WORDLE

How to Play
Guess the word in SIX tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word. Press ENTER/RETURN to sumbit your guess.

After you submit your guess, the letter will change colors depending on how close your guess was to the answer.

The letter will display GREEN if the letter correct and in the correct spot.

The letter will display ORANGE if that letter is in the word but incorrect spot.

The letter will display GRAY if the letter is not included in the word.

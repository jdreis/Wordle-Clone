WORDLE

How to Play
Guess the word in SIX tries or less.

Each of your guesses must be a valid 5 letter word. Press ENTER or RETURN to sumbit your guess.

After you submit your guess, the color of the letter will change depending on how close your guess was to the correct answer.

The letter will display GREEN if the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.

The letter will display YELLOW if that letter is in the word but incorrect spot.

The letter will display GRAY if the letter is not included in the word.
